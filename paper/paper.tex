\begin{abstract}
Hydrodynamic simulations of self-gravitating disc-planet interactions are presented. The energy equation is solved with a simple cooling law 
such that $\beta = t_c\Omega_k$ is constant, where $t_c$ is the cooling time and $\Omega_k$ is the Keplerian frequency. 
The simulations are customized to examine the stability of gaps opened by massive planets as a function of $\beta$. 

\end{abstract}
